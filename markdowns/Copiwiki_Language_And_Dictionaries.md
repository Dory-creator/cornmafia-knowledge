COPIWiki - Language and Dictionaries
====================================

The Cornucopias founders are in the UK and USA. However, for consistency the website and COPIWiki have been written using American English spelling as it is more universal across the internet.

Therefore you will see words such as Color, Armor, Decentralization that are **American English** that would appear as spelling mistakes in a **British English** dictionary that would be expecting the spellings Colour, Armour and Decentralisation.
