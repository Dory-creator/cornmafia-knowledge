Kitchen
=======

The workstation necessary for [crafting](/gameplay/gameplay-pc/user-interface/crafting) meals. Players bring [crops](/gameplay/gameplay-pc/resources/crops) to begin the cooking process.

Cooking stations can be found in every [district](/gameplay/themed-zones/sectors/land-sector-districts). 

Chopping Board
--------------

The workstation used to chop fruit, vegetables and meat.

Hobs
----

The workstation used to fry or boil crops.

Oven
----

The workstation is used to bake ingredients.
