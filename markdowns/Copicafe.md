COPICafe

![](https://copiwiki.cornucopias.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F4046923609-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FPQmCVki2WHg9QcW9pdrX%252Fuploads%252FnV7438Fd2YJupYpgHQ0O%252FGitBook_Subheads_COPICafe.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Deee40f70-5462-47ae-acb4-248b8cbf20a6&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=be21b7d1&sv=2)COPICafe is a regular show created for the main Cornucopias YouTube channel, hosted by the two [Cornucopias Co-CEOs Rob Greig and Josh Jones](/the-company/founders).

The first episode ‚Äåaired on Saturday 26th March 2022 and has been positively received ever since.

In COPICafe, Rob and Josh discuss the latest events from within the Cornucopias Game. 

**Fun fact:** There were two other pilot episodes created. Things moved so fast in Cornucopias, that by the time both were ready to be published the content was already out of date.
