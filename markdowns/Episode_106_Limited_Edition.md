Episode 106 - Limited Edition
=============================

In Episode 106 of [COPICafe](/gameplay/community/copicafe) the Rob and Josh discuss various updates with Cornucopias. 

The upcoming sale will include jetpacks, backpacks, vehicles, and apparel, with pricing details not yet finalized. The sale will not release all items immediately but in phases. The team also discusses upcoming hats, including the Burger Barn Deluxe and Quantum Jack Avatar hats. 

They mention updates on nodes, node reward claiming, and jetpack testing. The team plans to involve the community this month and is working on defining performance metrics and implementing security features. 

* [**00:00:00**](https://youtube.com/watch?v=ePtf35qSEcA&t=0) In this section the hosts discuss the upcoming sale for Cornucopias. The sale, scheduled around mid October, will include various items, although pricing details are not yet finalized. The hosts also mention updates on nodes, node reward claiming, and jetpack testing. The conversation is interspersed with light banter and personal anecdotes.
* [**00:05:00**](https://youtube.com/watch?v=ePtf35qSEcA&t=300) In this section the speakers discuss the upcoming sale of various assets, including jetpacks, backpacks, vehicles, and apparel. The sale will be available on both Cardano and Base, with the same quantity offered on each chain.
* [**00:10:00**](https://youtube.com/watch?v=ePtf35qSEcA&t=600) In this section the speaker discusses upcoming sales items, specifically hats, in the game. The Burger Barn Deluxe and Quantum Jack Avatar hats will be available, with the latter being free and a common type. These hats are part of a larger variety of items being released in three different phases. The speaker expresses excitement about the sale and mentions that more details, including pricing and timing, will be shared soon. Additionally, the speaker mentions an update on nodes, which includes integrating game downloads into the launcher and improving the download experience. The goal is for players to eventually download the game from the launcher, resulting in significant savings. Other upcoming features include Pool Management functionality and full rewards distribution to all nodes.
* [**00:15:00**](https://youtube.com/watch?v=ePtf35qSEcA&t=900) In this section the team discusses the upcoming developments for the COPI Node project. They plan to involve the community this month by recruiting 10 to 20 pool operators and updating the performance dashboard. The team is also working on defining performance metrics, creating documentation, and implementing security features. The reward claiming feature for Base and Cardano is almost complete and is expected to be turned on soon. The team is gradually moving towards using COPI utility for asset sales and plans to have node rewards ready for the next sale. They also tested jetpacks, which help navigate around and have a recharge function, and three of them will be coming soon with different traits.
* [**00:20:00**](https://youtube.com/watch?v=ePtf35qSEcA&t=1200) In this section the creators express excitement about the new jetpack functionality, which allows players to reach previously inaccessible spots and discover new artwork and challenges. The creators also mention the presence of collision issues and bugs resulting from the new feature, but remain optimistic about resolving them. The question section of the video includes a query about the 12 zones in the game and their significance. The creators confirm that not all zones have been decided yet and that there are other options under consideration.
* [**00:25:00**](https://youtube.com/watch?v=ePtf35qSEcA&t=1500) In this section the creators discuss their plans for community involvement in deciding which of ten potential zone ideas to pursue next for their project. They mention the possibility of a vote for the community to choose their favorite options. Although they have a clear idea for Zone Four, they have not finalized all decisions and will reveal more details later. Lastly, they reveal a potential new manufacturer leak.
* [**00:30:00**](https://youtube.com/watch?v=ePtf35qSEcA&t=1800) In this section the speaker introduces two new corn character designs called "corn cuties." These designs are backpacks, with one named "Grizzly Brown Brawler" and the other "Grizzly Purple Fury." Both are medium-sized backpacks from the same manufacturer and will be for sale. The speaker expresses their affection for the manufacturer's unique and appealing designs, despite the characters only having one tooth each. They also mention the possibility of more unique corn characters being released in the future. The video then concludes with the speaker expressing their excitement about playing a game and signing off.
