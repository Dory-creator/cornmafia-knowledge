Public Transport
================

Public transport is a service players use to connect them to the metaverse. This is a flying bus which arrives at regular intervals and links the Sector Hubs, [Themed Zones](/gameplay/themed-zones) and [The City](/gameplay/city-life).

The city has its own type of public transport - the city bus.
