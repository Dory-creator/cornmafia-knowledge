Plants
======

Plants are an essential crafting resource in Cornucopias. This resource can be used to craft clothing and technology within Cornucopias. 

The life cycle begins in raw forms scattered across themed zones, such as cotton and hemp. The cotton and hemp is then taken to the tailors to be processed into cotton cloth and linen cloth at the loom. Cloth can then be used to create clothing at a tailors table. 

Raw Plants
----------

Raw plants are a crafting resource which can be harvested through the Themed Zones. This can be gathered from plants in the form of cotton or hemp.

Processed Plants
----------------

Processed plants are crafting resources obtained by converting raw fabrics at a loom or [tailors station](/gameplay/themed-zones/sectors/land-sector-districts/district-workshops/tailors). For instance, cotton cloth, linen cloth and rope.
